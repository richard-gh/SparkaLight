What is a board?
A board is used to express our journey as well as discovering and exploring other people journeys.

How do I create a board?
  • 1 Click on create which is located at the top left side of each page.
  • 2. Choose a name and tell us abit about your board
  • 3. (Optional) You may wish to add your first picture on the board.
  • 4 . Click create when you are finished.

    •  How to add a picture to your own board
  • 1. Go to your board
  • 2. Click on add picture which is located above your board heading.
  • 3. Choose an image and write a description
  • 4, Click save when you're finish.

    How to delete a picture from a board
  • 1. Go to your Board
  • 2. Click edit board which is located above the heading of your board.
  • 3.Scroll down to locate the heading "Delete Picture From Board"
  • 4. Choose a board and click list.
  • 5. Click on the picture you want to delete.

    How to delete a board
  • 1. Click on edit profile , located under your profile description at your profile page
  • 2. Under the heading "Delete Board"
  • 3. Choose a board you would like to delete
  • 4, Click delete when you're finish.
